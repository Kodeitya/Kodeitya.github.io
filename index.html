About Me

Hello! I'm Aditya Kumar, a second-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Lovely Professional University. My passion for technology and innovation has led me to delve deeply into the world of web development, where I'm constantly exploring new ways to create engaging and efficient web experiences.

Since starting my journey in computer science, I've developed a strong foundation in programming and problem-solving. My coursework has provided me with a solid understanding of algorithms, data structures, and software engineering principles. However, it was my curiosity and enthusiasm for web technologies that truly sparked my interest and drove me to specialize in web development.

Over the past year, I've been honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with frameworks like React and Node.js. I love bringing ideas to life through code, and I'm always eager to learn new techniques and tools to improve my craft. Some of my projects include creating calculator, creating an interactive portfolio site, developing a small e-commerce site.

In addition to my technical skills, I enjoy collaborating with others and learning from different perspectives. Working on team projects and participating in hackathons have taught me the importance of communication, adaptability, and continuous learning.

When I'm not coding, you can find me playing chess, exploring new tech blogs, or reading books. These activities help me stay balanced and bring fresh ideas into my work.

I'm excited to continue my journey in web development and contribute to innovative projects that make a difference. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate on future projects. You can contact me via Email or connect with me on LinkedIn.
